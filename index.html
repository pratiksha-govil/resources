Is Dots right for you?

Choosing the right tool for your workflow

A dashboard is enough if…
  • You only track KPIs
  • Most data is quantitative
  • Your team already knows why trends are happening
A research repository is enough if…
  • You run occasional studies
  • Only one team consumes the findings
  • Insights do not need ongoing monitoring
Dots becomes essential when…
  • You work across 2+ datasets or data sources
  • You operate across countries or regions
  • You collect both qualitative and quantitative data
  • Insights need to be available at every team level
  • Reporting is repetitive and time-consuming
  • Leadership wants evidence behind every metric
